# v0.0.17.x series

This version series came as a minor update to 0.0.16.0 and added some features, as well as improved some areas of the kernel.

***

## <mark style="color:$primary;">Versions</mark>

Here are the versions that were released:

<details>

<summary>Version 0.0.17.0</summary>

This version was out on July 4, 2021.

#### <mark style="color:$primary;">Additions</mark>

* <mark style="color:green;">Added FaderBack</mark>
* <mark style="color:green;">WebAlerts support in mail has been added</mark>
* <mark style="color:green;">Added custom prompt styles for RSS, Text Edit, and ZIP shell</mark>

#### <mark style="color:$primary;">Improvements</mark>

* <mark style="color:yellow;">Fade-ins in Fader and FaderBack are now smoother</mark>
* <mark style="color:yellow;">Fixed "Invalid path" when trying to execute a non-existent mod/alias that contains the space in their command.</mark>
* <mark style="color:yellow;">Improvements regarding the RSS feed parser</mark>
* <mark style="color:yellow;">General improvements and bug fixes</mark>

</details>
